Interactive-Immersive LMS for Medical Students

Challenge: Traditional medical education often lacks hands-on, risk-free environments for practicing complex procedures and understanding intricate human anatomy.

Solution: Developed a cutting-edge AR/VR/MR-based Learning Management System. This platform allows medical students to perform virtual dissections, simulate surgeries, and explore anatomical structures in a fully immersive, interactive 3D environment.

Technologies: Unity 3D, ARKit/ARCore, Oculus SDK, Custom Backend (Node.js), 3D Modeling (Blender/Maya Integration).

Impact: Achieved a 30% improvement in student engagement and comprehension rates. Reduced reliance on expensive physical cadavers and lab equipment, making advanced medical training more accessible and cost-effective. Enhanced procedural recall and diagnostic accuracy for students.

Omni-Domain AI Chatbot for E-commerce

Challenge: An e-commerce giant faced overwhelming customer service inquiries, leading to slow response times, high operational costs, and declining customer satisfaction due to siloed support channels and language barriers.

Solution: Engineered a sophisticated, omni-domain, multimodal, and multilingual AI Chatbot. The bot seamlessly handles customer queries across web, mobile, and social platforms, supports multiple languages, and provides personalized recommendations and order assistance, freeing human agents for complex issues.

Technologies: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Google Cloud AI, Custom API Integration, Multimodal AI Frameworks.

Impact: Achieved a 45% reduction in customer support costs. Improved average response time by 70% and boosted customer satisfaction scores by 25%. Enabled 24/7 global customer support, significantly expanding market reach.

Custom ERP Solution with Predictive Analytics

Challenge: A large manufacturing client struggled with disconnected legacy systems for production, inventory, sales, and finance, leading to inefficiencies, data silos, and an inability to forecast demand accurately.

Solution: Developed a bespoke Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system integrated with advanced predictive analytics modules. This solution unified all core business functions, providing real-time data insights, automating workflows, and offering robust demand forecasting capabilities.

Technologies: Python (Django/Flask), PostgreSQL, Apache Kafka, Scikit-learn (for ML models), Tableau/Power BI Integration (for advanced reporting), AWS Cloud Infrastructure.

Impact: Delivered a 20% increase in operational efficiency and a 15% reduction in inventory holding costs through optimized forecasting. Enhanced decision-making speed for management, leading to improved resource allocation and supply chain management.

Secure Digital Wallet (Crypto↔Fiat) Platform

Challenge: A fintech startup aimed to launch a secure, user-friendly digital wallet supporting both cryptocurrency and traditional fiat transactions, but needed robust security, regulatory compliance, and seamless user experience.

Solution: Developed a full-featured digital wallet platform with advanced security protocols (multi-factor authentication, end-to-end encryption), integrated with multiple cryptocurrency exchanges and traditional banking rails.
